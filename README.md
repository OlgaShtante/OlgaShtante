### 🧪 Philosophy, briefly

- **"100% unit coverage" promises nothing** on its own — without integration tests, it's just green pixels.
- **"Unit e2e test" isn't a thing.** End-to-end means a real user flow — every integration included, nothing mocked. If it's mocked, it's not e2e; it's something else.
- **Integration tests are never mocked.** You can't verify an integration works against a fake end.
- **Automation isn't about replacing testers** — it's about freeing them to think and explore.
- **If QA is the bottleneck, the process is broken** — not the QA.
